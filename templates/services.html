EICR

The Electrical Installation Condition Report or the EICR is a certificate that details the condition of all the electrical installations and fittings in a property. The report is produced following an in-depth assessment carried out by a qualified electrician. A visual inspection of the installation is done and series of tests are complated to confirm that everything is functioning safely and is compliant with minimum regulatory standards. Where the electrician identifies any potentially hazardous defects, they will note this in the report with the appropriate recommendation code and advise the landlord of any required remedial work. The certificate will also list the date by which the next inspection is due.
