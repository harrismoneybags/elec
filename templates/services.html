Electrical Installation Condition Report

The Electrical Installation Condition Report (EICR), is a series of tests that are carried out to test the electrical health of your home, flat or business. starting at £75 (£60 per consumer unit and £15 per circuit there after). It's a legal requirement that the landlord has an EICR carried out every change of occupant, special rates can be established for customers that need multiple EICR completing or will be a repeat customer. The Report will be given to the client upon the completion of a visual inspection and set of strenuous tests that will result in a pass or remedial work that will need to be carried out within the 28 days period after the test has been carried out.
